Sara Cooper
I am a freelance journalist with a focus in health, environment & science reporting. I have particular expertise in data visualization and multimedia interactives.

In 2024 I entered Northwestern Medill Masters of Science Journalism program. Through my work I hope to communicate the importance of critical issues like climate change and sustainability while building an appreciation in others for science and nature.

Previous to my journalism education I have worked in public relations with a focus on data communication and visualization. As I expand on my journalism portfolio I'm avid to pursue stories that build on data to uncover truth.
